# leetgame — features

## Core Practice

**Verbal practice mode** — Given a LeetCode-style problem, explain your approach in plain English. The AI evaluates whether you understand the pattern, algorithm, and complexity — no code required.

**Stages** — Practice flows through up to five sequential stages, each building on the last:
- Edge Cases — identify boundary conditions
- Brute Force — describe a naive solution and its complexity
- Pattern — name the algorithmic pattern (sliding window, two pointers, etc.)
- Algorithm — explain the optimal approach step by step
- Time & Space Complexity — reason about the complexity of your solution

**Active stages** — Configure which stages you practice. Skip any you don't need (e.g. focus only on Pattern + Algorithm + T&SC).

**Hide title** — Optionally hide the problem title to drill recall without priming your memory.

## Problem Discovery

**Random practice** — Get a random problem to practice on.

**Search & filter** — Browse problems by keyword, difficulty (Easy / Medium / Hard), and topic tags. Results can be practiced as a sequential playlist.

**Smart practice** — The app picks the problem topic for you, weighted toward your weakest proficiency areas across your active topics and stages.

**Saved problems** — Bookmark any problem to revisit it later. Saved problems appear in the search page for quick access.

## Progress Tracking

**Topic proficiency** — After each practice session, your score per topic and stage is updated. The stats page shows a breakdown of every active topic, color-coded by score (red / yellow / green).

**Trend over time** — Expand any topic card in the stats page to see a line chart of your proficiency scores over the last 30 days, broken down by stage plus an overall average line.

**Active topics filter** — Choose which topics appear in smart practice and the stats page. Useful for focusing a study session on a subset of NeetCode topics.

**Daily streak** — A flame icon in the navbar tracks how many consecutive days you've practiced. Completing any session counts toward your streak.

## Account

**Authentication** — Sign in with email to persist settings and progress across devices. Anonymous practice is supported without an account; scores and settings are not persisted.
